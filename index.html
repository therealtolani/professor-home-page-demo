Biography

Omotola Eunice Sodiya is a Nigerian was born and brought up in the western part of the country.

-She studied Computer Science at the University of Lagos(2001-06).

-She got her Masters degree at Olabisi Onabanjo University in 2008.

-She got her Ph.D at Harvard University in 2010.

-She became a professor at Olabisi Onabanjo University lecturing Computer Science in 2017.
